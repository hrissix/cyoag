ABOUT

Raven Blossom has been last seen at Hollow Mansion, being busy and not responding to her family. At dusk a loud thunder-like noise is, heard followed by a scream. Raven’s relatives go to see what’s going on, they can’t find her. Her room is cold, windows are opened, looks like a tornado came through it, some of her personal belongings are missing as well. But there is blood on the floor and a haunting anonymous letter.

A young investigator is called to help - Spencer Hawk. She is asked to figure out what happened to Raven. She only has 24 hours to find out the truth. Spencer begins by taking a look at the crime scene, then interviews each member of the family. Afterwards Spencer investigates each of the clues she’s gathered.
